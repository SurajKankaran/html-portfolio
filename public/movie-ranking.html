My Favourite Movies of All Time

Top 3 Of Them Are Listed Below

URI:The Surgical Strike

This movie is based on an actual military operation.
The perfect military operation was undertaken by the
Indian Army to eliminate Pakistani terrorists.

Major

Another movie based on an actual incident that took place
in the Taj Hotel, Mumbai. This movie beautifully shows
how the black cat commandos eliminated the terrorists that
attacked the Taj Hotel and saved many lives.

Shershaah

Based on the Indo-Pak war . Also tells the love story of
Late Captain Vikram Batra who was a part of this war .
